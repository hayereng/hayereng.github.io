From humble beginnings nearly 30 years ago, Hayer Engineering has established itself as one of the leading precision engineering and custom machining firms in the region. With a core focus on the energy industry, Hayer delivers precision engineering solutions to some of the biggest providers in the world. We offer one-stop-shop, turnkey solutions as well as free material consignment, machining-only solutions.

Hayer moved into its current state-of-the-art facility in 2008. Located in the Jurong industrial district of Singapore, the facility spans over 8,700 square meters (94,000 square feet).

We achieved ISO9001:2008 certification in 1996, API Spec Q1 certification in 2013, and bizSAFE Level 3 certification in 2014. Hayer has also implemented stringent internal quality, environmental, and safety policies.

We are committed to delivering high quality products and services to all of our clients. Hayer strives to continuously improve its productivity, efficiency, and service.
