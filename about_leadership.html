Mr Soh Boon Hock - Executive Chairman

Mr Soh Boon Hock founded Hayer Engineering Pte Ltd in 1990. Mr Soh started his career in precision engineering and custom machining with Hughes Tool Company nearly 40 years ago. There's always a need for high-quality, consistent engineering solutions. Seeing an opportunity to provide such service in the oil & gas industry, Mr Soh went on to found Hayer Engineering.

Mr Soh's passion, vision, and expertise in engineering solutions permeates throughout Hayer Engineering. He has guided the firm to become one of the most respected and trusted engineering partners in the region.



Mr Soh Gin Hoo - Director

Mr Soh Gin Hoo joined Hayer Engineering as Director in 2012. Prior to Hayer, Mr Soh served 35 years as a public servant in Singapore's Civil Service across a number of Ministries. At Hayer, Mr Soh lends his expertise in government affairs while also heading the safety committee and assisting with management and human resource matters.

Mr Soh holds a Bsc degree in Mathematics from Nanyang University (now Nanyang Technological University).
